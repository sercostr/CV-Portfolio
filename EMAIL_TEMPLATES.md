## Quick Tips

‚úÖ **DO:**

- Respond within 24 hours
- Customize the template with specific company/role details
- Include 3-4 quantifiable achievements
- Keep it concise (150-200 words max)
- Always include your portfolio link
- Proofread for typos

‚ùå **DON'T:**

- Send generic copy-paste responses
- Write more than 250 words in initial email
- Forget to personalize recruiter's name
- Include salary requirements unless specifically asked
- Attach large PDF files in first email (portfolio link is better)
- Use overly casual language

---

## Response Time Guidelines

- **LinkedIn Message:** Within 12 hours
- **Email from Recruiter:** Within 24 hours
- **Follow-up after Interview:** Within 24 hours
- **Follow-up if No Response:** After 5-7 business days

---

## Customization Checklist

Before sending, consider:

- [ ] Add recruiter's name if known (optional)
- [ ] Add company name if you want to personalize (optional)
- [ ] Mention specific technologies from the job posting if relevant
- [ ] Add 1-2 company-specific details if you researched them
- [ ] Verify all links work
- [ ] Double-check your contact information
- [ ] Adjust technical details to match role requirements
